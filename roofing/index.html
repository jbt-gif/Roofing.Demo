Root Cause
Why roofing companies miss calls

Crew is on a roof

Hands full, environment loud, phone on silent. Structurally impossible to answer every inbound call while on the job.

After-hours calls hit voicemail

35% of inbound calls happen outside 8am–5pm. Homeowners call when they're home. You're closed.

No follow-up system

Missed calls become a mental note that never gets worked. The lead calls a competitor within 15 minutes.
